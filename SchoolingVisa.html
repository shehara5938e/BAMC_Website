Schooling Visa

Government Schooling via VIC

Our comprehensive schooling visa process ensures a smooth transition for students seeking to study in government schools in Victoria, Australia. We begin with an initial consultation to understand your academic goals and preferences, allowing us to tailor our services to your specific needs. Collaborating with the Victorian Department of Education, we find available schools that match your requirements.

Once a suitable school is identified, we assist with the preparation and submission of necessary documents to secure a letter of offer. We guide you through the visa application process, ensuring all forms are correctly filled out and submitted, thus increasing your chances of approval. Additionally, we provide pre-departure orientation and post-arrival support to help you settle into your new school smoothly.

Our Services For Schooling

  • Helping choose the right school for you.
  • Assistance with forms and document submission.
  • Guidance on visa application procedures and requirements.
  • Support for both students and parents during the transition period.
